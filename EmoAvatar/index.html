Abstract

The ACM Multimedia 2025 Grand Challenge of Avatar-based Multimodal Empathetic Conversation focuses on developing advanced AI systems capable of understanding and generating empathetic responses in multimodal dialogue contexts.

Task 1: Multimodal-Aware Empathetic Response Generation
Participants are required to develop a model capable of processing multimodal dialogue contexts and generating textual responses. Our team AI4AI achieved 3rd place in this task.

Task 2: Multimodal Empathetic Response Generation
Participants are required to develop a model capable of processing multimodal dialogue contexts and generating empathetic multimodal responses. Our team AI4AI achieved 2nd place in this task.

This challenge addresses the growing need for AI systems that can understand human emotions and respond appropriately in multimodal conversations, combining text, audio, and visual information to create more natural and empathetic human-AI interactions.
